An app that helps dog lovers plan dog-friendly adventures.

Project Overview

PROBLEM:

Dog owners have a hard time finding dog-friendly travel information. They are often overwhelmed by the searching work and prepare work before taking their dog for a trip.


THE GOAL

PawTRIP makes searching for dog-friendly travel information more efficient, fun, and effective.

MY ROLE:

UX Designer(discovery, user research, design, testing)
